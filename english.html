A cozy neighborhood restaurant

A warm Italian-style restaurant in Onjuku with handmade pizza, pasta, wine, and seasonal comfort-food favorites.

CABIN Pizza House blends a laid-back local atmosphere with a menu that feels heartfelt and homemade. The restaurant’s style is approachable, family-friendly, and full of personality.

The menu includes classic pizzas like Margherita and Pepperoni, plus seafood, curry pizza, pasta, salads, desserts, and drinks. It’s the kind of place where guests remember both the food and the atmosphere.
